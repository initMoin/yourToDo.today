Privacy Policy

toDō is built around clarity, control, and restraint. Data created in the app is meant to remain with the user and under the user’s control.

Effective date: April 1, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how toDō handles information across its website, app experiences, and related services.

The core principle is straightforward: toDō is designed to minimize data collection and keep user-created task data as close to the user as possible.

2. App data

toDō task data, notes, timing, recurrence details, organization, and related in-app content are stored on the user’s device or devices.

toDō does not treat user task content as a marketing asset, does not sell that data, and does not use it for advertising profiles.

3. Website mailing list

If a user submits an email address through the toDō website mailing list form, that email address may be used to send product updates, release notes, news, and related communication about toDō.

Mailing list information is used for direct communication related to the product and brand. It is not sold.

4. Sign-in and connected services

toDō may use sign-in or consent-based account services, including Google, to support account access, identity verification, synchronization, and related platform functionality.

When those services are used, only the information reasonably necessary to provide the relevant feature should be handled.

5. Cross-platform services

toDō may operate across iOS, web, Android, and other supported platforms. Where account-based sync or platform continuity is required, backend infrastructure such as Supabase may be used to support those features.

In those cases, information may be processed or stored to provide synchronization, account access, session management, and related product functionality. The aim remains the same: handle only what is needed to run the feature properly.

6. Technical processing and service operations

toDō is designed to minimize external data handling. Where website, authentication, synchronization, security, or related service functionality is used, limited technical or account-related information may be processed as necessary to operate those features.

This may include information needed for session handling, delivery, reliability, abuse prevention, and service security. toDō does not sell personal information and does not use task content for advertising profiles.

7. Data control

Users remain responsible for the content they create in toDō and retain control over that content within the product experience.

Users may also choose whether to join the mailing list and may unsubscribe from those communications when available through the mailing system in use.

8. Data sharing

toDō does not sell personal information.

Information may be shared only where needed to operate the product, comply with legal obligations, protect users, protect the service, enforce terms, or work with service providers supporting product functionality.

9. Policy changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the product evolves. The version posted on this page is the version in effect.

10. Contact

Questions related to privacy or data handling can be sent to support@iamshift.dev
